# Doc-Tags Procedure

Doc-tags are QR codes or URLs accessible on any mobile device used to document specific events and activities that take place in the store. These digital forms help maintain consistent record-keeping and ensure important information is captured accurately.

## What are Doc-Tags?

Doc-tags provide a quick and standardized way to document store activities using mobile devices. Each doc-tag links to a specific form designed to capture relevant information about particular events, incidents, or procedures.

## How to Use Doc-Tags

### Accessing Doc-Tags
1. **QR Code**: Use your mobile device's camera to scan the QR code
2. **Direct URL**: Navigate directly to the provided URL on any device
3. **Mobile Friendly**: All doc-tag forms are optimized for mobile device use

### General Process
1. Identify the situation that requires documentation
2. Locate the appropriate doc-tag (QR code or URL)
3. Scan the code or navigate to the URL on your mobile device
4. Fill out the form completely and accurately
5. Submit the form as instructed

## Best Practices

1. **Immediate Documentation**: Use doc-tags as soon as possible after an event occurs
2. **Complete Information**: Fill out all required fields accurately
3. **Clear Descriptions**: Use specific, factual language when describing events
4. **Privacy Considerations**: Be mindful of customer privacy when documenting incidents
5. **Follow-Up**: Ensure submitted forms are reviewed by management when required

## Technical Requirements

- Mobile device with camera (for QR codes)
- Internet connection
- No special apps required - works with standard web browsers

## Support

If you experience technical difficulties with doc-tags:
