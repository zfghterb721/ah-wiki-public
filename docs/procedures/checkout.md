# Checkout

Efficiently scan products or add cards to a person's cart to get them checked out. If there is not a small line feel free to converse with a customer and ask if they need anything else/ did they find anything they were looking for. If the line gets backed up, reduce small talk and work on getting the customer checked out. This said, there is no rush by trying to speed up a process you will make errors that will end up taking longer than if you were to just go at your own pace. The longer a customer stays in line the more likely they are to buy something else.

# Adding Names

Its very important to get names attached to as many orders as possible the data we get from this helps use better serve our customers. Also it helps with the refund process and pre orders. It will also be a good way for our customers to obtain rewards. Before finishing a purchase make sure to add the customers name to the checkout if they have an account. As seen below. If they don’t have an account ask them to make one quickly, if they refuse check them out with no name. To make an account you will need a customers first and last name and a working email address.

# Priory in Check Out

Always prioritize a sale over a buylist. If a customer comes to checkout while you are working on buying singles, save the cart then check out the customer, pull up the saved cart and continue with the buylist.

# Using multiple credit cards per a transaction

Binder POS allows a customer to pay with cash, credit card and store credit and can use a combination of these payments to pay for their purchase. Unfortunately it does not allow for more than card to be used per a cart. If the customer wants to split the purchase between two or more cards you will have to make separate transactions for each card.

# Card Minimum

The minimum for a card transaction is $3 to help customers reach this minimum offer that they buy a booster pack or if it is snacks that they buy a snack card. When a customer uses a snack card, scan the items and set the cost to zero then make sure you add their name to the cart.

# Missing Barcodes/Product in system

If a barcode is missing from a product try and find the product manually. Then note the product and fix the barcode in the Monroe Shopify as soon as possible. If the product cannot be found manually mark the product down for the manager to create the product then run the product as a miscellaneous transaction.

# Store Credit Transfer

Store credit is usable between all A&H Games locations but not all accounts are link for each location. So if a customer wants to use their store credit from the Monroe location at the Ruston location they will need a credit transfer. If a customer wants to perform a credit transfer all you have to do is call the shop they want the credit moved from and ask for the amount they have, add that to their store credit and the other store will subtract that amount from their account.
