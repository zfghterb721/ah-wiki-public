# Operations

This section provides an overview of how A&H Games functions as a business.

Operations focuses on how decisions are made, how different parts of the store work together, and how employees should approach their responsibilities beyond basic procedures.

---

## Purpose

The goal of this section is to help employees understand:

- How the store operates as a whole  
- Why certain policies and procedures exist  
- How to make good decisions in situations not explicitly covered elsewhere  

---

## Core Principles

- **Consistency**  
  Customers should receive the same experience regardless of who is working.

- **Clarity**  
  Clear communication prevents confusion and mistakes.

- **Accountability**  
  Employees are responsible for their actions and decisions.

- **Professionalism**  
  Employees represent A&H Games at all times while on shift.

- **Customer Experience**  
  Every decision should support a positive and welcoming environment.

## Notes

- Procedures explain *what to do*  
- Policies explain *what is allowed*  
- Operations explains *how to think*  

If a situation is not clearly covered, use these principles to guide your actions and involve management when needed.
