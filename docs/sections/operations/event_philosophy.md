# Event Philosophy

This document outlines the principles behind how events are run at A&H Games.

The goal is to create events that are organized, enjoyable, and welcoming to players of all experience levels.

---

## Core Goal

Events should be:

- Fun  
- Fair  
- Welcoming  
- Well-organized  

A successful event is not just about competition—it is about creating a positive experience for everyone involved.

---

## Community First

Events are a core part of building the A&H Games community.

- Encourage new players to participate  
- Support returning players  
- Create an environment where people want to come back  

Every event should contribute to a stronger, more inclusive community.

---

## Accessibility

Events should be approachable for all players.

- Be patient with new or inexperienced players  
- Explain formats and expectations clearly  
- Avoid creating an intimidating atmosphere  

Employees should help lower barriers to entry whenever possible.

---

## Fairness and Consistency

- Apply rules consistently to all players  
- Make clear and unbiased rulings  
- Follow announced prize structures  

Fairness builds trust in the event and the store.

---

## Structure and Organization

- Start events on time  
- Keep rounds moving  
- Communicate clearly throughout the event  

A well-run event feels smooth and professional.

---

## Player Experience

- Be attentive to player needs  
- Address issues quickly  
- Maintain a positive environment  

Even competitive events should feel welcoming and respectful.

---

## Family-Friendly Standard

All events must follow A&H Games’ family-friendly expectations.

- Monitor language and behavior  
- Address issues immediately  
- Ensure the environment is appropriate for all ages  

This standard applies at all times, including during competitive play.

---

## Balance Competition and Fun

- Encourage good sportsmanship  
- Prevent overly aggressive or toxic behavior  
- Keep the focus on enjoyment, not just winning  

Players should want to return regardless of their record.

---

## Employee Role

Employees are responsible for:

- Running events smoothly  
- Enforcing rules and policies  
- Supporting players  
- Maintaining the store environment  

Employees are not just organizers—they set the tone for the entire event.

---

## Important Notes

- Do NOT prioritize speed over player experience  
- Do NOT ignore behavior issues  
- Do NOT allow inconsistent rule enforcement  

When in doubt, prioritize fairness and the overall experience.

---

Great events build strong communities. Every event at A&H Games should reflect professionalism, fairness, and a welcoming atmosphere.
