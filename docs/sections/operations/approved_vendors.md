# Approved Vendors

This document defines which vendors may be used for purchasing goods and services for A&H Games.

Only approved vendors may be used.

---

## Core Rule

Employees may not purchase, order, or source items from any vendor not listed in this document.

This applies to:
- Inventory
- Store supplies
- Event materials
- Equipment
- Services

Unauthorized purchasing is not allowed under any circumstance.

---

## Authorization

Only authorized personnel may make purchases.

Employees may not:
- Make purchases without approval
- Use personal judgment to select vendors
- Substitute vendors without permission

All purchasing decisions must follow this document and management direction.

## Vendor Restrictions

Employees may not:

- Use unlisted vendors
- Create new vendor relationships
- Accept unsolicited vendor offers
- Purchase from individuals without approval
- Circumvent approved vendors for convenience

---

## Exceptions

Any exception requires manager approval.

Employees must:
- Ask before acting
- Provide clear reasoning

Employees may not:
- Assume approval
- Make one-time exceptions

---

## Cost Awareness

All purchasing decisions must consider:

- Necessity
- Cost efficiency
- Impact on the business

Convenience is not a valid reason to bypass standards.

---

## Prohibited Behavior

Employees may not:

- Make unauthorized purchases
- Use personal funds without approval
- Seek reimbursement for unapproved purchases
- Select vendors based on preference instead of policy

These are policy violations.

---

## Default Action Rule

If an employee is unsure:

1. Do not make the purchase
2. Ask a manager

No purchase should occur without clear authorization.

---

Approved vendor usage ensures consistency, cost control, and operational stability across both locations.
