# First Day

This guide outlines what to expect on your first day at A&H Games.

The goal of your first day is to get comfortable in the store, understand basic expectations, and begin learning how things work.

---

## Your Focus

On your first day, focus on:

- Getting familiar with the store layout  
- Observing how tasks are performed  
- Learning how to interact with customers  
- Asking questions  

You are not expected to know everything right away.

---

## What You Will Do

- ☐ Meet your manager and team members  
- ☐ Get a basic tour of the store  
- ☐ Learn where key areas are (register, inventory, play space)  
- ☐ Observe how transactions are handled  
- ☐ Watch how employees interact with customers  

---

## Customer Interaction

You may begin interacting with customers in simple ways:

- Greet customers  
- Answer basic questions if you are comfortable  
- Ask another employee for help if unsure  

**Suggested response:**  
"Let me check on that for you."

---

## Register and Systems

- Observe how the POS system is used  
- Do not worry about memorizing everything  
- Ask questions as you watch  

You will learn systems over time.

---

## Expectations

- Be present and attentive  
- Ask questions when unsure  
- Stay engaged and observe  

It is okay to not know something — it is not okay to guess.

---

## What Not to Worry About

- You are not expected to know all products  
- You are not expected to run the register alone  
- You are not expected to handle difficult situations yet  

Focus on learning.

---

## Important Notes

- Follow instructions from your manager  
- Refer to procedures when needed  
- Ask before making decisions you are unsure about  

---

Your first day is about learning and getting comfortable. Take your time, ask questions, and focus on understanding how the store operates.
