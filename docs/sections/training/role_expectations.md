# Role Expectations

This document outlines what is expected of employees during every shift at A&H Games.

Employees are responsible for maintaining a consistent, professional, and customer-focused environment.

---

## Core Responsibilities

During every shift, employees are expected to:

- Assist customers  
- Process transactions accurately  
- Maintain store cleanliness and organization  
- Follow all policies and procedures  

---

## Customer Focus

- Greet customers as they enter  
- Be available and attentive  
- Provide accurate and helpful information  

Customers are the top priority at all times.

---

## Register Accuracy

- Enter all items correctly  
- Verify totals before completing transactions  
- Follow pricing and discount policies  

Do not rush. Accuracy is critical.

---

## Store Awareness

- Stay aware of the entire store  
- Monitor both retail and play areas  
- Address issues as they arise  

Do not become distracted or isolated.

---

## Cleanliness and Organization

- Keep the counter area clean  
- Maintain shelves and displays  
- Ensure play areas are tidy  

There is always something to clean or organize.

---

## Following Procedures

- Use procedures for all tasks  
- Do not improvise or skip steps  
- Ask if unsure  

Consistency is required.

---

## Professional Conduct

- Be respectful to customers and coworkers  
- Use appropriate language  
- Maintain a family-friendly environment  

You represent A&H Games while on shift.

---

## Time Management

- Be on time and ready to work  
- Stay productive during your shift  
- Prioritize tasks based on store needs  

Avoid idle time when work is available.

---

## Decision Making

- Follow store policies  
- Do not make exceptions without approval  
- Ask a manager when unsure  

Consistency is more important than convenience.

---

## Accountability

- Take responsibility for your actions  
- Correct mistakes when they occur  
- Be open to feedback  

---

## What to Avoid

- Ignoring customers  
- Guessing information or pricing  
- Making exceptions to policy  
- Standing idle when work is available  

---

## Important Notes

- You are not expected to know everything  
- You are expected to follow procedures and ask questions  
- Consistency and professionalism are key  

---

Meeting these expectations ensures a positive experience for customers and supports the overall success of A&H Games.
