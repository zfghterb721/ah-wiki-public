# First Week

This guide outlines what to focus on during your first week at A&H Games.

The goal of your first week is to begin performing core tasks, build confidence, and develop consistency in your role.

---

## Your Focus

During your first week, focus on:

- Performing basic tasks with guidance  
- Learning store procedures  
- Building confidence with customer interactions  
- Understanding daily store flow  

You are still learning. Accuracy and consistency are more important than speed.

---

## Core Skills to Develop

### Customer Interaction

- ☐ Greet customers consistently  
- ☐ Answer basic questions  
- ☐ Ask for help when unsure  

---

### Register Basics

- ☐ Begin ringing up simple transactions  
- ☐ Verify items and totals carefully  
- ☐ Follow pricing and discount policies  

Do not rush. Take your time and ask questions.

---

### Store Awareness

- ☐ Stay aware of customers in the store  
- ☐ Keep the store clean and organized  
- ☐ Assist when needed  

---

### Procedures

- ☐ Begin using opening and closing checklists  
- ☐ Follow step-by-step procedures when completing tasks  
- ☐ Ask if anything is unclear  

---

## Product Familiarity

- Learn basic product categories:
  - TCGs  
  - Board games  
  - Accessories  

- Understand common customer needs:
  - Packs vs singles  
  - Sleeves and deck boxes  
  - Beginner vs experienced players  

You do not need to know everything—focus on the basics.

---

## Decision Making

- Follow store policies  
- Do not guess or improvise  
- Ask a manager when unsure  

Consistency is more important than trying to solve everything on your own.

---

## Common Situations

During your first week, you may encounter:

- Customers asking for recommendations  
- Basic transactions  
- Questions about products  

Use simple responses and ask for help when needed.

**Suggested response:**  
"Let me check on that for you."

---

## Expectations

- Be engaged and attentive  
- Ask questions regularly  
- Apply what you’ve learned  
- Stay professional  

---

## What Not to Do

- Do NOT guess answers or pricing  
- Do NOT make exceptions to policy  
- Do NOT rush through tasks  

---

## Important Notes

- Mistakes may happen — learn from them  
- It is better to slow down than to be incorrect  
- Asking questions is expected  

---

Your first week is about building confidence and consistency. Focus on doing things correctly and learning how the store operates day-to-day.
