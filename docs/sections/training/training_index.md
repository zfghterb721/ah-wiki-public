# Training

This section provides a structured path for onboarding and developing employees at A&H Games.

Training is designed to help employees become confident, consistent, and capable in their role.

---

## Purpose

Employees should be able to:

- Understand store expectations  
- Perform daily tasks correctly  
- Interact with customers confidently  
- Apply policies and procedures consistently  

Training is an ongoing process, not a one-time event.

### Ongoing Development

- Continue reviewing Procedures and Policies  
- Build product knowledge over time  
- Ask questions and seek clarification when needed  

---

## How to Use This Section

- Follow the training path in order  
- Review materials before and after shifts  
- Use Procedures for step-by-step guidance  
- Use Operations for decision-making support  

---

## Expectations

- Be engaged and willing to learn  
- Ask questions when unsure  
- Apply what you learn during shifts  
- Take responsibility for your development  

---

## Important Notes

- You are not expected to know everything immediately  
- Accuracy and consistency are more important than speed  
- It is better to ask than to guess  

---

Training ensures that every employee is prepared to contribute to a consistent and high-quality experience at A&H Games.
