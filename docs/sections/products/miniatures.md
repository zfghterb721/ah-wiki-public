# Miniatures Basics

This document provides an overview of miniature-based games and related products.

Miniatures are a specialized part of the hobby and often involve collecting, building, and painting models used in tabletop gameplay.

---

## What are Miniatures?

Miniatures are small physical models used in tabletop games.

They are typically:
- Assembled from kits  
- Painted by the player  
- Used to represent units or characters in a game  

Common examples include games like Warhammer and other tabletop wargames.

---

## Types of Miniature Products

### Model Kits

- Unassembled miniatures  
- Require building (and often glue or tools)  
- May require painting  

---

### Prebuilt / Prepainted Models

- Ready to use out of the box  
- No assembly required  

---

### Paints and Supplies

- Paints  
- Brushes  
- Tools (clippers, glue, etc.)  

These are essential for assembling and customizing models.

---

## How Miniature Games Work

Most miniature games involve:

- Building an army or collection  
- Moving models on a tabletop battlefield  
- Using rules to resolve actions and combat  

These games are often:
- More time-intensive  
- More complex than typical board games  

---

## Helping Customers

Ask simple questions:

- "Are you building or playing?"  
- "Do you already have a faction or army?"  
- "Are you looking for models, paints, or tools?"  

---

## Basic Recommendations

### New Customers

- Explain that models may require assembly and painting  
- Recommend starter sets if available  
- Suggest basic tools and paints  

---

### Returning Players

- Help locate specific models or supplies  
- Assist with accessories and hobby needs  

---

## Important Notes

- Do NOT assume models are ready to use  
- Do NOT recommend products without understanding what they are for  
- Do NOT guess compatibility between systems  

If unsure, ask another employee or check product details.

---

Miniatures can be one of the most rewarding parts of the hobby. Helping customers understand what they need improves their experience and builds long-term engagement.
