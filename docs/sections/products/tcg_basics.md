# TCG Basics

This document provides an overview of Trading Card Games (TCGs) and how to assist customers with common questions.

TCGs are one of the core product categories at A&H Games.

---

## What is a TCG?

A Trading Card Game (TCG) is a game where players build decks of cards and compete against other players.

Common examples include:
- Magic: The Gathering  
- Pokémon  
- Yu-Gi-Oh!  
- Disney Lorcana  

Players collect cards, build decks, and play matches using game-specific rules.

---

## Main Product Types

### Sealed Product

Sealed product includes:

- Booster packs  
- Booster boxes  
- Starter decks / preconstructed decks  

These are unopened products sold by the manufacturer.

**Why customers buy sealed product:**
- To open packs and collect cards  
- To build or improve decks  
- For fun or gambling-style excitement  

---

### Singles

Singles are individual cards sold separately.

**Why customers buy singles:**
- To get specific cards they need  
- To improve a deck efficiently  
- To avoid randomness of packs  

---

## Common Terms

- **Deck** — A set of cards used to play  
- **Pack / Booster** — A randomized set of cards  
- **Rarity** — Indicates how common or rare a card is  
- **Meta** — Popular or competitive strategies in the game  

You do not need to know every term, but you should recognize common ones.

---

## Helping Customers

When assisting with TCG products:

- Ask what game they play  
- Ask what they are trying to do (build, collect, compete)  
- Identify experience level  

**Suggested questions:**

- "What game are you playing?"  
- "Are you looking to build a deck or just open packs?"  
- "Do you know what cards you need?"  

---

## Basic Recommendations

### New Players

- Recommend starter or preconstructed decks  
- Avoid overwhelming them with advanced options  

### Casual Players

- Recommend packs or fun products  
- Help them explore different options  

### Competitive Players

- Direct them toward singles  
- Help them find specific cards  

---

## Important Notes

- Do NOT assume what a customer wants  
- Do NOT recommend expensive options without understanding their goal  
- Do NOT guess card values or rulings  

If unsure, ask another employee or look it up.

---

Understanding the basics of TCGs allows you to confidently assist customers and improve their experience at A&H Games.
