# Accessories Guide

This document provides an overview of common gaming accessories and how to recommend them to customers.

Accessories support gameplay, protect products, and improve the overall experience.

---

## What are Accessories?

Accessories are add-on items used alongside games or cards.

Common examples include:

- Card sleeves  
- Deck boxes  
- Dice  
- Playmats  
- Storage solutions  

---

## Common Accessory Types

### Card Sleeves

Used to protect trading cards from damage.

- Recommended for all TCG players  
- Available in different sizes depending on the game  

**When to recommend:**
- Customer is buying cards or building a deck  
- Customer wants to protect valuable cards  

---

### Deck Boxes

Used to store and transport decks.

- Keeps cards organized and protected  

**When to recommend:**
- Customer is buying sleeves or cards  
- Customer mentions building a deck  

---

### Playmats

Used as a clean surface for playing.

- Protects cards from rough or dirty tables  
- Often features artwork  

**When to recommend:**
- Customer plays regularly  
- Customer is interested in improving their setup  

---

### Dice

Used in many games for tracking or gameplay.

- Common in TCGs and tabletop games  

**When to recommend:**
- Customer is playing games that require tracking (life totals, counters, etc.)  

---

### Storage

Includes binders, boxes, and organizers.

- Used for storing collections  

**When to recommend:**
- Customer has a large number of cards  
- Customer is organizing or collecting  

---

## Helping Customers

Ask simple questions:

- "Do you need anything to go with that?"  
- "Do you have sleeves for your cards?"  
- "Are you storing or playing with these?"  

---

## Basic Recommendations

- Buying cards → recommend sleeves  
- Building a deck → recommend sleeves + deck box  
- Playing regularly → recommend playmat  
- Collecting → recommend storage  

---

## Important Notes

- Do NOT assume the customer needs accessories  
- Do NOT push unnecessary items  
- Do NOT recommend incorrect sizes or incompatible products  

If unsure, ask or verify before recommending.

---

Accessories improve both gameplay and product longevity. Recommending the right items helps customers get the most out of their purchases.
