# Sealed vs Singles

This guide explains the difference between sealed products and singles, and how to help customers choose between them.

Understanding this distinction is essential for making good product recommendations.

---

## Sealed Product

Sealed product includes:

- Booster packs  
- Booster boxes  
- Starter or preconstructed decks  

These products are unopened and contain randomized or pre-built cards.

### When to Recommend Sealed

- The customer wants to open packs for fun  
- The customer is collecting cards  
- The customer is new and wants a starter deck  
- The customer enjoys randomness or surprises  

### Key Points

- Results are not guaranteed  
- Pulling specific cards is unlikely  
- Value varies from pack to pack  

---

## Singles

Singles are individual cards sold separately.

### When to Recommend Singles

- The customer needs specific cards  
- The customer is building or improving a deck  
- The customer wants guaranteed value  
- The customer is playing competitively  

### Key Points

- No randomness  
- Exact cards can be chosen  
- More efficient for deck building  

---

## How to Help Customers Decide

Ask simple questions:

- "Are you looking for specific cards or just opening packs?"  
- "Are you building a deck or collecting?"  

---

## General Guidance

- If the customer wants specific cards → recommend singles  
- If the customer wants fun or variety → recommend sealed  
- If the customer is new → recommend starter decks  

---

## Important Notes

- Do NOT promise specific cards from sealed product  
- Do NOT recommend sealed when a customer clearly needs singles  
- Do NOT assume the customer’s goal  

Always match the recommendation to what the customer is trying to do.

---

Helping customers choose correctly improves their experience and builds trust in A&H Games.
