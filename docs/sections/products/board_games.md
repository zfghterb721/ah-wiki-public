# Board Game Basics

This document provides an overview of board games and how to help customers find the right game.

Board games are a key part of creating a welcoming, family-friendly environment at A&H Games.

---

## What is a Board Game?

Board games are tabletop games played with physical components such as boards, cards, dice, and pieces.

They are typically:
- Self-contained (everything needed is in the box)  
- Designed for groups of players  
- Focused on fun, strategy, or social interaction  

---

## Key Factors to Consider

When helping a customer, focus on these:

### Number of Players

- How many people will be playing?  
- Some games are best for small groups, others for larger groups  

---

### Experience Level

- New or casual players  
- Experienced players  

Avoid recommending complex games to beginners unless requested.

---

### Game Length

- Short (15–30 minutes)  
- Medium (30–60 minutes)  
- Long (60+ minutes)  

Customers often care about how long a game takes.

---

### Complexity

- Light (easy to learn, quick to play)  
- Medium (some strategy, moderate rules)  
- Heavy (complex rules, deep strategy)  

Keep recommendations appropriate for the customer’s comfort level.

---

## Common Types of Board Games

### Party Games

- Simple rules  
- Designed for groups  
- Focus on fun and interaction  

### Strategy Games

- More planning and decision-making  
- Often longer play time  

### Family Games

- Easy to learn  
- Suitable for a wide range of ages  

---

## Helping Customers

Ask simple questions:

- "How many people will be playing?"  
- "Are you looking for something quick or more involved?"  
- "Is this for family, friends, or a specific group?"  

---

## Recommendations

### New Customers / Families

- Recommend easy-to-learn games  
- Focus on fun and accessibility  

### Casual Groups

- Suggest party or mid-weight games  
- Balance fun and strategy  

### Experienced Players

- Suggest more complex or strategic games  
- Ask about preferred mechanics or styles  

---

## Important Notes

- Do NOT recommend overly complex games to beginners  
- Do NOT assume customers want long games  
- Do NOT guess rules or details  

If unsure, ask another employee or check the product.

---

Helping customers find the right board game creates a positive experience and encourages them to return to A&H Games.
