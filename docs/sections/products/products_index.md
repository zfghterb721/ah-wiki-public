# Products

This section provides guidance on the types of products sold at A&H Games and how to assist customers with product-related questions.

The goal is to help employees confidently identify products, understand basic differences, and make helpful recommendations.

---

## Purpose

Employees should be able to:

- Recognize major product categories  
- Understand basic use cases for products  
- Help customers find what they need  
- Ask the right questions when assisting customers  

You are not expected to know everything, but you should be able to guide customers effectively.

## Helping Customers

When assisting customers:

- Ask what they are looking for  
- Identify their experience level  
- Recommend appropriate products  
- Keep explanations simple  

**Suggested questions:**

- "What game are you playing?"  
- "Are you looking for something competitive or casual?"  
- "Is this for you or as a gift?"  

---

## Important Notes

- Do NOT guess product details  
- Do NOT recommend items you don’t understand  
- If unsure, ask another employee or look it up  

---

Product knowledge improves over time. Focus on learning the basics and building confidence through experience.
