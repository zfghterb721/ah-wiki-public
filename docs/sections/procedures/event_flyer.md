# Event Flyer

A significant part of our advertising for events is through word of mouth, but having a physical sheet of paper that people can reference is also extremely important to have people remember events. At each store there is a monthly flyer that must be available and distributed to customers, ideally a week before the new month starts. 

Based upon the number of participants, etc. the event may be used/adapted.

Once the events have been made/approved for the next month, they are available in a printable form here: 

Ruston

[https://ah-games-store.github.io/rack-card-calendar/?store=Ruston&month=5](https://ah-games-store.github.io/rack-card-calendar/?store=Ruston&month=5)

Monroe

[https://ah-games-store.github.io/rack-card-calendar/?store=Monroe&month=5](https://ah-games-store.github.io/rack-card-calendar/?store=Monroe&month=5)

If one pays attention to the link, you can see that the **Month** and the **Store** must be specified to access the correct sheet. By filling in "store=" with **Monroe** or **Ruston**, you can change the location, and by specifying "month=" with the number of the month, the rack card can be accessed.


The current format for printing is a normal 8x11 sheet of paper, in the future this may change.
