# Event Teardown

Follow these steps after an event has concluded to properly close out the event and restore the store.

---

## Final Results

- ☐ Confirm final standings are correct  
- ☐ Resolve any remaining disputes  
- ☐ Ensure all results are entered and saved  

---

## Prize Distribution

- ☐ Distribute prizes according to the announced structure  
- ☐ Confirm recipients and amounts  
- ☐ Process prizes through POS system (if applicable)  

Do not deviate from the announced prize structure.

---

## Event Closeout

- ☐ Close out event in event software (if applicable)  
- ☐ Ensure event is fully completed and recorded  

---

## Play Area Cleanup

- ☐ Remove trash from tables  
- ☐ Straighten tables and chairs  
- ☐ Return play space to normal layout  

---

## Store Reset

- ☐ Return any moved furniture or displays  
- ☐ Ensure retail area is organized  
- ☐ Restock items if needed  

---

## Supplies and Materials

- ☐ Collect and store event supplies  
- ☐ Put away any shared materials  

---

## Final Check

- ☐ Ensure store is clean and presentable  
- ☐ Confirm no items are left behind  

---

## Important Notes

- Do NOT delay prize distribution unnecessarily  
- Do NOT leave event materials or mess for later  
- Do NOT deviate from prize policy  

If any issues arise, notify a manager.

---

Proper event teardown ensures the store remains clean, organized, and ready for customers and future events.
