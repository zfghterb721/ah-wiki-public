# Doc-Tags

Doc-tags are QR codes or URLs accessible on any mobile device used to document specific events and activities that take place in the store.

These digital forms help maintain consistent record-keeping and ensure important information is captured accurately.

---

## What are Doc-Tags?

Doc-tags provide a quick and standardized way to document store activities using mobile devices.

Each doc-tag links to a specific form designed to capture relevant information about particular events, incidents, or procedures.

---

## How to Use Doc-Tags

### Accessing Doc-Tags

- **QR Code:** Use your mobile device's camera to scan the QR code  
- **Direct URL:** Navigate directly to the provided URL  
- **Mobile Friendly:** All forms are optimized for mobile use  

---

### General Process

1. Identify the situation that requires documentation  
2. Locate the appropriate doc-tag (QR code or URL)  
3. Scan or navigate to the link on your mobile device  
4. Fill out the form completely and accurately  
5. Submit the form as instructed

## Best Practices

- Document events as soon as possible  
- Fill out all required fields completely  
- Use clear, factual descriptions  
- Be mindful of customer privacy  
- Ensure forms are reviewed when required  

---

## Technical Requirements

- Mobile device with camera (for QR codes)  
- Internet connection  
- No special apps required (works in browser)
