# Handling Customer Issues

Follow these guidelines when dealing with customer complaints, disputes, or difficult situations.

The goal is to resolve issues calmly, consistently, and in accordance with A&H Games policies.

---

## General Approach

- ☐ Stay calm and professional  
- ☐ Listen to the customer without interrupting  
- ☐ Acknowledge the concern  
- ☐ Respond clearly and respectfully  

Do not take complaints personally.

---

## Clarify the Issue

- ☐ Ask questions if needed  
- ☐ Confirm what the customer is requesting  
- ☐ Identify the relevant policy or procedure  

Make sure you fully understand the situation before responding.

---

## Apply Policy

- ☐ Refer to store policies when making decisions  
- ☐ Explain the policy clearly and simply  
- ☐ Apply rules consistently  

Do not make exceptions without approval.

---

## Communicate Clearly

- ☐ Be direct and confident  
- ☐ Avoid arguing or debating  
- ☐ Do not over-explain  

**Suggested response:**  
"I understand what you’re asking, but we have to follow our store policy."

---

## Common Situations

### Pricing or Discounts

- ☐ Confirm price in POS system  
- ☐ Explain pricing policy  
- ☐ Do not negotiate  

**Suggested response:**  
"Prices are set and consistent for all customers."

---

### Returns or Exchanges

- ☐ Verify eligibility  
- ☐ Follow return procedure  
- ☐ Explain policy if needed  

---

### Buylist / Trade-Ins

- ☐ Evaluate items in front of the customer  
- ☐ Explain offer clearly  
- ☐ Do not hold items for later  

---

### Behavior Issues

- ☐ Address behavior immediately  
- ☐ Reinforce expectations  
- ☐ Escalate if necessary  

---

## De-escalation

If a customer becomes upset:

- ☐ Remain calm and composed  
- ☐ Lower your tone if needed  
- ☐ Focus on resolving the issue, not “winning”  

**Suggested response:**  
"I want to help resolve this. Let’s figure out the best way forward."

---

## When to Involve a Manager

- Customer requests an exception to policy  
- Customer becomes aggressive or hostile  
- Situation is unclear or unusual  
- High-value transaction or dispute  

Do not hesitate to involve a manager.

---

## Removal from Store

If a situation escalates:

- ☐ Clearly state expectations  
- ☐ Ask the customer to leave if necessary  
- ☐ Follow customer conduct policy  

**Suggested response:**  
"I need you to leave the store if this behavior continues."

---

## Important Notes

- Do NOT argue with customers  
- Do NOT make promises you cannot keep  
- Do NOT ignore issues hoping they resolve themselves  

Consistency is more important than trying to satisfy every request.

---

Handling issues professionally protects employees, maintains store standards, and ensures a consistent experience for all customers.
