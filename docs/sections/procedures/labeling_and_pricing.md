# Labeling and Pricing

Follow these steps to ensure all inventory is labeled and priced accurately and consistently.

Proper labeling ensures clarity for customers and prevents pricing disputes.

---

## General Expectations

- All items must have a clear and accurate price  
- Labels must be easy to read and correctly placed  
- Pricing must match the POS system  

If a discrepancy exists, the POS system is the source of truth.

---

## Labeling Items

- ☐ Apply a price label if the item does not already have one  
- ☐ Ensure the label is legible and properly attached  
- ☐ Place the label in a consistent, visible location  

Avoid placing labels over important product information when possible.

---

## Verifying Prices

- ☐ Confirm price matches the POS system  
- ☐ Double-check high-value items  
- ☐ Ensure sale or promotional pricing is correct  

Do not guess or estimate pricing.

---

## Pricing Consistency

- ☐ Ensure identical items are priced consistently  
- ☐ Verify shelf price matches register price  
- ☐ Correct any mismatches immediately  

Customers should not encounter conflicting prices.

---

## Updating Prices

- ☐ Replace outdated or incorrect labels  
- ☐ Remove old pricing when updating  
- ☐ Confirm new pricing is reflected in POS  

Do not leave multiple or conflicting price labels.

---

## Special Cases

### Trading Card Game Singles

- ☐ Verify card and condition  
- ☐ Ensure correct price is applied  
- ☐ Confirm label matches system pricing  

### High-Value Items

- ☐ Double-check pricing accuracy  
- ☐ Ensure items are properly secured  
- ☐ Confirm label is clearly visible  

---

## Missing Prices

If an item does not have a price:

- ☐ Look up item in POS system  
- ☐ Inform the customer of the correct price  
- ☐ Label the item as soon as possible  

Do not sell items without confirming price.

---

## Important Notes

- Do NOT manually adjust prices at the register without approval  
- Do NOT honor incorrect labels without manager approval  
- Do NOT leave items unlabeled  

If a pricing issue arises, pause and involve a manager.

---

## Customer Interaction

If a pricing discrepancy occurs:

- Stay calm and professional  
- Verify price in the system  
- Explain clearly and politely  

**Suggested response:**  
"Let me double-check that price in our system to make sure it's correct."

---

Accurate labeling and pricing maintain customer trust and ensure smooth transactions.
