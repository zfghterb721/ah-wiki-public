# Closing Checklist

Complete the following steps to properly close and secure the store.

---

## Before Closing

- ☐ Announce closing time to customers (10–15 minutes prior)  
- ☐ Ensure no new games or transactions are started close to closing  
- ☐ Begin light cleaning and organizing  

---

## Customer Exit

- ☐ Ensure all customers have completed purchases  
- ☐ Ask remaining customers to finish up and exit  
- ☐ Confirm all customers have left the store  

---

## Register Closeout

- ☐ Complete all transactions in POS  
- ☐ Count cash drawer  
- ☐ Verify totals match expected amounts  
- ☐ Report any discrepancies to management  
- ☐ Secure cash as required  

---

## Store Cleanup

- ☐ Remove trash from play areas  
- ☐ Empty trash bins (if needed)  
- ☐ Clean and straighten tables and chairs  
- ☐ Return misplaced items to proper locations  

---

## Retail Area

- ☐ Straighten shelves and displays  
- ☐ Ensure high-value items are secured  
- ☐ Confirm all product is properly stored  

---

## Systems & Equipment

- ☐ Log out of POS system  
- ☐ Shut down computers (if required)  
- ☐ Turn off music  

---

## Final Walkthrough

- ☐ Check bathrooms for cleanliness and issues  
- ☐ Ensure all areas are clean and organized  
- ☐ Confirm no customers remain in the store  

---

## Lock Up

- ☐ Turn off all lights (leave security lights on)  
- ☐ Set thermostat appropriately  
- ☐ Lock all exterior doors  
- ☐ Set alarm (if applicable)  

---

## After Closing

- ☐ Do not remain in store after hours without authorization  
- ☐ Report any issues to management  

---

Closing procedures must be completed every night to ensure store security and readiness for the next business day.
