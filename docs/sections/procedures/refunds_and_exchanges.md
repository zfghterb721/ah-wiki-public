# Refunds and Exchanges

Follow these steps to process refunds and exchanges in accordance with A&H Games policy.

---

## Before Starting

- ☐ Ask for a receipt or proof of purchase  
- ☐ Confirm the item was purchased from A&H Games  
- ☐ Verify the item is eligible for return or exchange  

If unsure, pause and ask a manager before proceeding.

---

## Verify Eligibility

- ☐ Confirm item meets return policy requirements  
- ☐ Check condition of the item  
- ☐ Confirm return timeframe (if applicable)  

Items that do not meet policy requirements should not be processed.

---

## Inspect Item

- ☐ Ensure item is in acceptable condition  
- ☐ Confirm all components are present (if applicable)  
- ☐ Check for damage, wear, or use  

If the item is not in acceptable condition, do not proceed without manager approval.

---

## Determine Resolution

### Refund

- ☐ Confirm refund method (original payment method if possible)  
- ☐ Verify refund amount matches original purchase  

### Exchange

- ☐ Identify replacement item  
- ☐ Confirm price difference (if any)  
- ☐ Process exchange accordingly  

---

## Process in POS

- ☐ Select correct return/exchange function  
- ☐ Enter or scan returned item  
- ☐ Apply refund or exchange  
- ☐ Confirm transaction is completed  

---

## Receipts

- ☐ Provide receipt for refund or exchange  
- ☐ Ensure transaction is properly recorded  

---

## Customer Interaction

- ☐ Remain calm and professional  
- ☐ Clearly explain the process if needed  
- ☐ Refer to store policy when questions arise  

**Suggested response:**  
"I want to make sure we’re following our return policy correctly. Let me check on that for you."

---

## When to Involve a Manager

- No receipt or proof of purchase  
- Item does not meet return conditions  
- Customer disputes the policy  
- Unusual or high-value return  
- Any situation you are unsure about  

---

## Important Notes

- Do NOT override policy without approval  
- Do NOT issue refunds for ineligible items  
- Do NOT guess — ask if unsure  

---

Accurate handling of refunds and exchanges ensures fairness, protects store inventory, and maintains customer trust.
