# Receiving Inventory

Follow these steps when receiving and processing incoming inventory.

All inventory must be checked, verified, and properly stored before being made available for sale.

---

## Upon Arrival

- ☐ Receive shipment from carrier or vendor  
- ☐ Check for visible damage to boxes or packaging  
- ☐ Confirm shipment matches expected delivery (if known)  

If there is significant damage, notify a manager before opening.

---

## Open and Inspect

- ☐ Open boxes carefully  
- ☐ Remove items and packing materials  
- ☐ Inspect items for damage or defects  

Do not discard packaging until items are verified.

---

## Verify Contents

- ☐ Compare items to packing slip or order  
- ☐ Confirm quantities match  
- ☐ Identify any missing or incorrect items  

If discrepancies are found:
- Set affected items aside  
- Notify a manager  

---

## Check Condition

- ☐ Ensure items are in sellable condition  
- ☐ Look for damage, defects, or tampering  

Do not place damaged items into inventory without approval.

---

## Enter into System

- ☐ Add items to POS or inventory system (if required)  
- ☐ Confirm correct product, quantity, and pricing  
- ☐ Ensure items are properly tracked  

Accuracy is critical.

---

## Labeling

- ☐ Apply price labels if needed  
- ☐ Ensure labels are correct and readable  

Follow store labeling and pricing procedures.

---

## Storage and Placement

- ☐ Place items in correct location (shelves, back stock, etc.)  
- ☐ Organize neatly and consistently  
- ☐ Ensure high-value items are secured  

Do not leave inventory unattended or unorganized.

---

## Clean Up

- ☐ Dispose of packaging materials  
- ☐ Break down boxes as needed  
- ☐ Keep receiving area clean  

---

## Important Notes

- Do NOT leave inventory unprocessed  
- Do NOT assume quantities are correct without checking  
- Do NOT mix new inventory with old stock until verified  

If unsure at any step, ask a manager.

---

Proper inventory handling ensures accurate stock levels, prevents loss, and keeps the store organized and ready for customers.
