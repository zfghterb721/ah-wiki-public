# Opening Checklist

Complete the following steps to prepare the store for opening.

---

## Before Opening Doors

- ☐ Unlock front door  
- ☐ Turn off alarm (if applicable)  
- ☐ Turn on all store lights (leave security lights as-is)  
- ☐ Set thermostat to appropriate temperature  

---

## Register Setup

- ☐ Log into POS system  
- ☐ Confirm register is operational  
- ☐ Ensure receipt paper is loaded  
- ☐ Verify cash drawer is balanced and ready  

---

## Store Readiness

- ☐ Turn on computers, monitors, and necessary systems  
- ☐ Ensure internet is working  
- ☐ Check that music (if used) is appropriate and at reasonable volume  

---

## Retail Area

- ☐ Walk the floor and check for cleanliness  
- ☐ Straighten shelves and displays  
- ☐ Return misplaced items to proper locations  
- ☐ Ensure high-value items are properly secured  

---

## Play Area

- ☐ Check tables and chairs are organized  
- ☐ Remove trash or leftover items  
- ☐ Ensure play space is clean and usable  

---

## Inventory & Supplies

- ☐ Check for any items that need restocking  
- ☐ Verify bags, sleeves, and commonly used items are stocked at the counter  

---

## Final Check

- ☐ Ensure front area is clean and presentable  
- ☐ Confirm register area is organized  
- ☐ Be ready to greet customers  

---

## Open Store

- ☐ Unlock doors (if not already unlocked)  
- ☐ Be at the counter and ready for customers at opening time  

---

Opening procedures should be completed before customers begin entering the store. If any issues arise during opening, notify management as soon as possible.
