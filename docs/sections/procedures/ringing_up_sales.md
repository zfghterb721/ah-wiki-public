# Ringing Up Sales

Follow these steps to process customer purchases accurately and consistently.

---

## Start Transaction

- ☐ Greet the customer  
- ☐ Scan or locate each item in the POS system  
- ☐ Verify the correct item and price appear  

---

## Verify Items

- ☐ Confirm quantity of each item  
- ☐ Check condition if applicable (especially singles)  
- ☐ Ensure all items are included before proceeding  

---

## Confirm Total

- ☐ State the total clearly to the customer  
- ☐ Confirm payment method (cash, card, etc.)  

---

## Discounts and Pricing

- ☐ Apply discounts only if permitted by policy  
- ☐ Do NOT adjust prices manually without approval  
- ☐ Do NOT negotiate or change prices based on payment method  

**Important:**
- Cash payments do NOT qualify for discounts  
- Prices are the same regardless of payment method  

**Suggested response:**  
"Prices are the same regardless of payment method, but I’m happy to help you find something within your budget."

---

## Process Payment

### Card

- ☐ Run card through POS system  
- ☐ Wait for approval  
- ☐ Confirm transaction is completed  

### Cash

- ☐ Enter amount received  
- ☐ Count change back to the customer  
- ☐ Place cash in drawer properly  

---

## Receipts

- ☐ Offer a receipt to the customer  
- ☐ Provide printed or digital receipt as requested  

---

## Bagging and Completion

- ☐ Bag items if needed  
- ☐ Return items and receipt to customer  
- ☐ Thank the customer  

---

## After Transaction

- ☐ Ensure register area is clean and organized  
- ☐ Prepare for next customer  

---

## Notes

- Always follow pricing and discount policies  
- When in doubt, ask a manager before completing the sale  
- Accuracy is more important than speed  

---

Consistent and accurate transactions help maintain trust with customers and ensure smooth store operations.
