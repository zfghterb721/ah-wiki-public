# Processing Trade-Ins / Buylist

Follow these steps when purchasing items from customers.

All buylist transactions must be handled accurately, transparently, and immediately.

---

## Important Policy

- Items must be evaluated and processed **while the customer is present**  
- Do NOT take items into the back or out of the customer’s view  
- Do NOT hold or store customer items for later evaluation  
- Do NOT ask customers to leave items for later processing  

All transactions must be completed in real time with the customer.

This protects both the customer and the store from misunderstandings or disputes.

---

## Start Transaction

- ☐ Greet the customer  
- ☐ Ask what items they are looking to sell  
- ☐ Confirm items are eligible for purchase  

If unsure whether items are eligible, ask a manager before proceeding.

---

## Evaluate Items

- ☐ Inspect item condition  
- ☐ Verify authenticity (if applicable)  
- ☐ Confirm item is allowed for purchase  

All evaluation must be done:
- At the counter  
- In clear view of the customer  

---

## Determine Value

- ☐ Look up item pricing in the system  
- ☐ Confirm buylist value (cash and/or store credit)  
- ☐ Clearly communicate offer to the customer  

Do not guess pricing. Always use the system or approved tools.

---

## Confirm Agreement

- ☐ Ensure the customer agrees to the offer  
- ☐ Clarify payment method (cash, check, or store credit)  

If the customer declines, return all items immediately.

---

## Identification Requirement

- ☐ Record valid driver's license for cash transactions  

This is required for all cash buylist transactions.

Do not proceed without proper identification.

---

## Process in POS

- ☐ Enter items into the buylist system  
- ☐ Confirm totals match agreed offer  
- ☐ Complete transaction  

---

## Payment

### Cash / Check

- ☐ Issue payment according to policy  
- ☐ Ensure documentation is complete  

### Store Credit

- ☐ Apply credit correctly in POS  
- ☐ Confirm customer receives credit  

---

## Finalize Transaction

- ☐ Provide receipt or documentation  
- ☐ Transfer items into store inventory  
- ☐ Thank the customer  

---

## Important Notes

- Do NOT process your own buylist transactions  
- Do NOT take items into private areas  
- Do NOT delay or postpone evaluation  
- Do NOT deviate from pricing or policy  

If any issue arises, stop and involve a manager.

---

## Suggested Response (If Customer Asks to Leave Items)

"I’m sorry, but we’re not able to hold or take items for later. We need to evaluate and process everything while you’re here."

---

Proper handling of buylist transactions ensures fairness, protects the store, and maintains trust with our customers.
