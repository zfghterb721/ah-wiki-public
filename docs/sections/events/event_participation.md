# Event Participation Policy

This policy outlines expectations for all customers participating in events at A&H Games.

Our goal is to provide a fun, fair, and welcoming environment for all players, regardless of experience level.

---

## General Expectations

All participants are expected to:

- Follow event rules and formats  
- Treat other players and staff with respect  
- Maintain appropriate language and behavior  
- Follow all store policies while participating  

Participation in events is a privilege and may be revoked if expectations are not met.

---

## Sportsmanship

Players are expected to demonstrate good sportsmanship at all times.

- Be respectful to opponents  
- Accept wins and losses appropriately  
- Avoid arguments, hostility, or unsportsmanlike conduct  
- Follow judge or staff rulings without disruption  

Disputes should be handled calmly and directed to event staff when necessary.

---

## Family-Friendly Environment

All events must maintain the store’s family-friendly standard.

- Excessive or inappropriate profanity is not permitted  
- Conversations and behavior should be appropriate for all ages  
- Employees are responsible for addressing violations during events  

Failure to follow these expectations may result in removal from the event or store.

---

## Following Staff Instructions

Event participants must follow instructions from employees and event staff.

- This includes pairings, timing, rulings, and general event flow  
- Failure to follow instructions may result in penalties or removal  

---

## Time Management

Players are expected to:

- Arrive on time for events  
- Be ready for each round when called  
- Complete matches within the allotted time  

Repeated delays or slow play may result in penalties at staff discretion.

---

## Use of Store Space

Participants must respect the shared play environment.

- Keep play areas reasonably clean  
- Do not leave excessive clutter on tables  
- Be mindful of space and other players  

Food and drinks must be handled responsibly.

---

## Cheating and Fair Play

Cheating is strictly prohibited.

This includes, but is not limited to:
- Manipulating game states dishonestly  
- Misrepresenting rules or outcomes  
- Collusion between players  

Any suspected cheating should be reported to event staff immediately.

Confirmed cheating will result in removal from the event and may result in further action.

---

## Removal from Events

Employees have the authority to remove participants from an event for:

- Violating store policies  
- Disruptive or inappropriate behavior  
- Failure to follow staff instructions  

Removal from an event may occur without refund.

---

## Bans and Escalation

If a participant is removed from an event:

- Notify a manager as soon as possible  
- Management will determine whether a ban is necessary  
- Management will determine the duration and terms of any ban  

---

## Consistency

These rules apply to all participants equally.

- Do not make exceptions for regulars or experienced players  
- Address issues early before they escalate  

---

A&H Games events are designed to be enjoyable, fair, and welcoming for everyone. All participants are expected to contribute to that environment.
