# Event Teardown

Event teardown must be completed immediately after the event concludes. The store must be returned to normal operating condition without delay.

Employees are responsible for ensuring all materials, space, and records are properly handled before leaving the event.

Teardown is not optional and may not be deferred.

---

## Core Rules

- Teardown must begin **immediately after prize distribution**
- The event area must be **fully reset before employees move to other tasks**
- All event materials must be **accounted for and secured**
- All results must be **finalized before teardown is complete**

Employees may not:

- Leave the event area in disarray
- Delay cleanup
- Leave materials unsecured
- Skip required closing steps

---

## Finalization Before Teardown

Before beginning physical cleanup, employees must:

- Confirm all match results are entered
- Verify final standings are correct
- Distribute all prizes according to structure
- Resolve all disputes

Employees may not:

- Begin teardown before results are finalized
- Distribute prizes after teardown has started

---

## Table and Space Reset

Employees must:

- Clear all tables used for the event
- Remove trash, scrap paper, and leftover materials
- Return tables and chairs to standard layout
- Wipe down surfaces if needed

If space was taken from casual players:

- Restore general play areas promptly

Employees may not:

- Leave tables cluttered
- Leave trash or debris behind
- Leave tables in event-specific configurations

---

## Materials and Supplies

Employees must collect and secure all event materials, including:

- Match slips
- Writing materials
- Event signage
- Timer devices
- Any reusable supplies

Employees must:

- Return materials to designated storage
- Report missing or damaged items

Employees may not:

- Leave materials on tables
- Discard reusable supplies
- Take materials home

---

## Product and Prize Handling

Employees must:

- Return unused product to inventory if applicable
- Verify all prize support was distributed correctly
- Secure any remaining event-related items

Employees may not:

- Leave product unattended
- Sell or redistribute leftover product without approval
- Take product for personal use

---

## Player Follow-Up

Before closing the event:

- Ensure all players have:
  - Received prizes (if applicable)
  - Cleared their space

Employees may:

- Answer final questions briefly

Employees may not:

- Allow players to linger in a way that disrupts operations

---

## Issue Reporting

Employees must note any issues that occurred, including:

- Disputes
- Player removals
- Rule enforcement issues
- Operational problems (delays, space issues, etc.)

Serious issues must be escalated to management immediately.

---

## Final Check

Before considering teardown complete, employees must confirm:

- [ ] Event results finalized
- [ ] Prizes fully distributed
- [ ] Tables cleared and reset
- [ ] Trash removed
- [ ] Materials collected and stored
- [ ] Product secured
- [ ] Issues noted or reported

---

## Non-Negotiable Rules

- No delaying teardown
- No leaving the event area unclean
- No unsecured materials or product
- No incomplete finalization

Failure to properly complete teardown is a performance issue.
