# Events

Events are a core part of store operations. They must be executed consistently, professionally, and without deviation from established rules and procedures.

This section defines how events are structured, prepared, executed, and enforced at A&H Games.

Employees are responsible for maintaining control of events at all times. Events are not casual free-for-alls — they are organized, structured experiences that reflect directly on the store.

---

## Core Principles

All event-related documents are built around the following non-negotiable principles:

- Events must be **fair and consistent**
- Rules must be **applied equally to all players**
- Employees must **enforce rules immediately**
- No employee may **modify event structure without approval**
- Customer experience must be **managed, not left to chance**
- Managers make **final rulings on disputes and edge cases**

## Enforcement Expectations

- Employees **must not ignore issues** during events
- Employees **must not improvise rules or structures**
- Employees **must not allow players to self-govern disputes**
- Employees **must intervene immediately** when problems arise
- Failure to enforce event standards is a **performance issue**

---

## Cross-Location Consistency

- Both store locations must run events **identically**
- No location-specific rule variations unless approved by management
- Event expectations must be **uniform across all stores**

---

## Final Authority

- Managers have final authority on:
  - Disputes
  - Rule interpretations
  - Player discipline
  - Event structure exceptions

Employees must escalate when:
- A situation is unclear
- A player becomes argumentative or disruptive
- A decision may impact fairness or store reputation

---

## Non-Negotiable Rules

- No unauthorized discounts tied to events
- No altering entry fees or prize structures
- No holding product for players
- No exceptions based on familiarity or customer status
