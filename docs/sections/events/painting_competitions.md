# Painting Competitions

Painting competitions must be structured, controlled, and resistant to manipulation. These events are highly subjective and must follow strict procedures with no deviation.

Employees are responsible for ensuring all submissions, posting, voting, and results are handled consistently and without bias.

---

## Core Rules

- All submissions must go through the **official submission form**
- No manual or informal submissions are allowed
- Voting must occur only through **approved platforms**
- Employees must follow **exact posting and formatting rules**
- Employees may not deviate from established procedures
- Employees must remain **completely neutral**

---

## Submission System (Required)

All painting competitions must use a Google Form.

### Required Fields

The form must collect:

- Name (kept private)
- Email address
- Submission title
- Product line used
- 1–3 image uploads

### Form Configuration

- Title field: **Required**
- Image uploads: **Required**
- Enable **email receipt confirmation**
- Include acknowledgment checkbox with the following text:

> "By submitting this form, you agree to A&H Games posting your submitted photo on our various social media platforms and our website. By submitting this form, you also acknowledge that your miniature and/or given title are not offensive, vulgar, or crude, and that A&H Games reserves the right to not post any photos submitted here (or via email) for any reason."

- Include checkbox:
  - "I confirm that I am a local area resident"

### Approved Template

- Previous form (reference only):
  - https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1QAppLYrBJUIXhRw5StyhQqvqPjKOOL9Kycda2hAoQmE

### Submission Rules

- Employees must not accept:
  - Email submissions
  - In-person submissions
  - Social media submissions
- Late submissions may not be accepted without manager approval
- Incomplete submissions must not be accepted

---

## Submission Review

Before posting, employees must:

- Verify images are:
  - Clear and viewable
  - Appropriate (no offensive, vulgar, or crude content)
- Verify title is appropriate
- Confirm submission meets all form requirements

Employees may reject submissions that violate standards.

---

## Discord Setup (Required)

A dedicated Discord channel must be created for each competition.

### Channel Rules

- Channel name: **Painting Contest Submissions**
- Channel must be set to **read-only for customers**
- Only staff may post

### First Post (Required)

- Must contain voting instructions:
  - Only **heart reactions count as votes**
  - Other reactions are allowed but do not count
- This post must be **pinned**

### Posting Submissions

- Each submission must be posted individually
- Each post must include:
  - Images from submission
  - Submission title only

Employees may not include:

- Participant name
- Commentary
- Additional text

---

## Facebook Setup (Required)

All submissions must be posted to Facebook.

### Album Rules

- All entries must be posted in a **single album**
- Album description must include voting instructions:
  - Only **heart reactions count as votes**
  - Other reactions are allowed but do not count

### Posting Rules

- Each submission must:
  - Use submitted images
  - Display submission title only

Employees may not:

- Add commentary
- Highlight specific entries
- Modify titles

---

## Voting Rules

Voting must occur only through:

- Discord reactions
- Facebook reactions

### Vote Counting

- Only **heart reactions count as votes**
- All other reactions are ignored

### Enforcement

Employees must:

- Monitor for suspicious activity
- Report concerns to management

Employees may not:

- Remove votes without manager approval
- Adjust vote totals
- Influence voting in any way

---

## Neutrality and Conduct

Employees must remain neutral at all times.

Employees may not:

- Suggest entries to vote for
- Promote specific submissions
- Share personal opinions during the voting period
- Participate in voting unless explicitly allowed

---

## Disqualification

Submissions may be disqualified if they:

- Contain offensive, vulgar, or crude content
- Violate submission rules
- Are found to be fraudulent or manipulated

Only a manager may approve disqualification.

---

## Finalization

After voting concludes:

- Votes must be counted accurately
- Results must be verified before announcement
- Winners must be announced consistently across platforms

Employees must not:

- Announce results early
- Modify outcomes
- Resolve ties without manager input

---

## Non-Negotiable Rules

- No manual submissions
- No deviation from posting format
- No altering vote counts
- No employee influence on results
- No exceptions for specific customers

Failure to follow these procedures is a performance issue.
