# Event Participation

All event participants must follow these rules at all times.

Participation in events is a privilege, not a right. Failure to meet these expectations may result in removal from the event or store.

Employees are responsible for enforcing these standards without exception.

---

## Core Rules

Participants must:

- Follow all event rules and formats
- Follow all store policies
- Follow all staff instructions immediately
- Maintain appropriate behavior at all times

Participants may not:

- Argue with staff rulings
- Disrupt the event
- Ignore instructions
- Create an uncomfortable environment for others

---

## Sportsmanship

Players must:

- Treat opponents and staff with respect
- Accept match results without argument
- Handle disputes through staff only

Players may not:

- Argue with opponents
- Attempt to resolve disputes without staff
- Display hostility or unsportsmanlike behavior

---

## Following Staff Instructions

Staff instructions must be followed immediately.

This includes:

- Pairings and seating
- Round timing
- Match procedures
- Rulings and decisions

Failure to follow instructions will result in penalties or removal.

---

## Time Expectations

Participants must:

- Arrive before the event start time
- Be present and ready when rounds begin
- Complete matches within the allotted time

Participants may not:

- Delay rounds
- Leave matches incomplete without notifying staff
- Repeatedly arrive late

---

## Family-Friendly Standard

All events must meet the store’s family-friendly expectations.

Participants may not:

- Use excessive or inappropriate profanity
- Engage in vulgar, crude, or offensive behavior
- Create an environment unsuitable for all ages

Employees will enforce these standards immediately.

---

## Use of Store Space

Participants must:

- Keep play areas clean
- Manage personal items responsibly
- Respect shared space

Participants may not:

- Leave excessive clutter
- Interfere with other matches
- Misuse store property

---

## Cheating and Fair Play

Cheating is strictly prohibited.

This includes:

- Misrepresenting game rules or outcomes
- Manipulating game state dishonestly
- Collusion between players

Any suspected cheating must be reported to staff immediately.

Confirmed cheating will result in immediate removal and escalation to management.

---

## Removal from Events

Participants may be removed for:

- Violating any event or store rules
- Disruptive or inappropriate behavior
- Failure to follow staff instructions
- Unsportsmanlike conduct

Removal may occur without warning depending on severity.

Removal from an event may occur without refund.

---

## Escalation and Bans

If a participant is removed:

- A manager must be notified
- Management determines further action, including bans

Participants may be removed from the store entirely if necessary.

---

## Consistency

These rules apply equally to all participants.

No exceptions will be made for:

- Regular customers
- Experienced players
- Personal relationships

---

## Non-Negotiable Rules

- Staff rulings are final
- Instructions must be followed immediately
- Disruptive behavior will not be tolerated
- Cheating results in removal
- No exceptions based on familiarity

Failure to follow these rules will result in removal from the event or further action.
