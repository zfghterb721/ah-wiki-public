# POS Overview

This document provides an overview of the Point of Sale (POS) system used at A&H Games.

The POS system is central to store operations and must be used for all transactions.

---

## Purpose

The POS system is used to:

- Process sales  
- Handle refunds and exchanges  
- Manage buylist / trade-ins  
- Track inventory  
- Record pricing  

The POS system is the **source of truth** for all pricing and transaction data.

---

## Core Principles

- Always use the POS system for transactions  
- Always verify information before completing a transaction  
- Never bypass the system or create workarounds  

---

## Pricing

- All prices come from the POS system  
- Do NOT manually adjust prices without approval  
- Do NOT guess or estimate pricing  

If a price appears incorrect, stop and ask a manager.

---

## Transactions

All sales must be processed through the POS system.

- Do NOT complete transactions outside the system  
- Do NOT skip steps or rush through entries  
- Ensure all items are correctly entered  

Accuracy is critical.

---

## Inventory

The POS system tracks inventory levels.

- Items must be entered correctly to maintain accuracy  
- Do NOT remove items from inventory without recording them  
- Do NOT leave transactions incomplete  

---

## Buylist / Trade-Ins

- All buylist transactions must be recorded in the POS  
- Values must match the system  
- Do NOT process trades outside the system  

---

## Refunds and Exchanges

- Must be processed through the POS system  
- Must follow return policy  
- Do NOT issue refunds manually  

---

## Important Notes

- Do NOT override the system without approval  
- Do NOT ignore system errors or warnings  
- Do NOT assume the system is correct without verification  

If something seems wrong, pause and ask a manager.

---

## If the System Is Down

- Notify a manager immediately  
- Do not attempt workarounds unless directed  
- Follow instructions provided by management  

---

Proper use of the POS system ensures accurate transactions, reliable inventory tracking, and consistent store operations.
