# QuickBooks Time (Timekeeping)

This document explains how employees track their work hours using QuickBooks Time.

All employees are required to accurately record their time for every shift.

---

## Purpose

QuickBooks Time is used to:

- Clock in and out of shifts  
- Track total hours worked  
- View scheduled shifts  

Accurate timekeeping ensures correct payroll and accountability.

---

## Access

Access QuickBooks Time at:

https://tsheets.intuit.com/?realm_id=193514843494199&from=time_center

Employees are responsible for logging in and managing their time.

---

## Clocking In

- ☐ Clock in at the exact start of your shift  
- ☐ Be present and ready to work before clocking in  

Do not clock in early unless instructed.

---

## Clocking Out

- ☐ Clock out immediately after your shift ends  
- ☐ Ensure all work is completed before clocking out  

Do not remain clocked in after leaving.

---

## During Your Shift

- ☐ Remain clocked in for the entire shift  
- ☐ Do not leave the store while clocked in without approval  

If you must leave, notify a manager.

---

## Accuracy

- ☐ Ensure all time entries are correct  
- ☐ Review your hours if needed  

Do not guess or estimate your time.

---

## Schedule

- ☐ Check your schedule regularly  
- ☐ Be aware of upcoming shifts  

Employees are responsible for knowing when they are scheduled.

---

## Issues and Corrections

If a mistake occurs:

- Notify a manager as soon as possible  
- Do not attempt to hide or ignore errors  

---

## Important Notes

- Do NOT clock in if you are not ready to work  
- Do NOT clock out late to add extra time  
- Do NOT allow someone else to clock in or out for you  

Falsifying time records may result in disciplinary action.

---

Accurate timekeeping is essential for payroll, scheduling, and overall store operations.
