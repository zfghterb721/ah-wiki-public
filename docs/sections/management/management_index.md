# Management

This section provides guidance for managers at A&H Games.

Managers are responsible for maintaining store standards, supporting employees, and ensuring consistent operations across all areas of the business.

---

## Purpose

Managers are expected to:

- Enforce policies and procedures consistently  
- Support and develop employees  
- Make decisions when situations are unclear  
- Maintain store operations and environment  

Managers are responsible for the overall performance of the store during their shift.

## Notes

- Managers are expected to lead by example  
- Managers must enforce policies consistently across all employees  
- Managers are responsible for maintaining a safe, professional environment

Management is responsible for upholding the standards of A&H Games and ensuring a consistent experience for both employees and customers.
