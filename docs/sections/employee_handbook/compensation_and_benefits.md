# Compensation and Benefits

This section defines how employees are compensated, how time is tracked, and how time off and reimbursements are handled at A&H Games.

Employees are responsible for following all requirements in this section.

Failure to follow these policies may result in disciplinary action.

---

## Payroll

Employees are paid on a weekly basis.

- Pay periods run Monday through Sunday  
- Payment is issued via direct deposit  
- Employees are responsible for ensuring their banking information is accurate  

---

## Timekeeping

All employees must accurately track their working hours using the Workforce app.

- Employees must clock in at the start of their shift  
- Employees must clock out at the end of their shift  
- Time records must reflect actual hours worked  

Employees are responsible for ensuring their time is properly recorded.

Falsification of time records is a violation of company policy and may result in immediate termination.

---

## Time Off

Time off must be approved by management.

Employees requesting time off must:

- Notify their manager at least one week in advance  
- Arrange for shift coverage when applicable  

Time off is not guaranteed until approved.

Unapproved absences may be treated as unexcused and may result in disciplinary action.

---

## Expense Reimbursement

Employees may be reimbursed for approved business-related expenses.

- All expenses must receive prior approval from a manager  
- Reimbursements may be issued via paycheck, cash, or check  
- Employees must retain receipts and provide documentation when requested  

Unapproved expenses will not be reimbursed.

---

These policies define how compensation, time tracking, and reimbursements are handled. Employees must follow these requirements at all times.
