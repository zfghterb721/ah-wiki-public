# Compensation and Benefits

This section outlines how employees are compensated and reimbursed at A&H Games, along with expectations regarding timekeeping and time off.

---

## Payroll

Employees are paid on a bi-weekly basis.

- Pay periods run Monday through Sunday  
- Payment is issued via direct deposit  
- Employees are responsible for ensuring their banking information is accurate  

---

## Timekeeping

All employees are required to accurately track their working hours using Quickbooks Time.

- Employees must clock in at the start of their shift  
- Employees must clock out at the end of their shift  
- Time records must be accurate and reflect actual hours worked  
- Falsification of time records is a violation of company policy and may result in disciplinary action  

Access Quickbooks Time here:  
https://tsheets.intuit.com/?realm_id=193514843494199&from=time_center

---

## Time Off

Employees requesting time off must:

- Notify their manager at least one week in advance  
- Arrange for shift coverage when applicable  

Time off is not guaranteed and must be approved by management.

Failure to follow the time-off request process may result in the absence being treated as unexcused.

---

## Expense Reimbursement

Employees may be reimbursed for approved business-related expenses.

- All expenses must receive prior approval from a manager  
- Reimbursements may be issued via paycheck, cash, or check  

Employees should retain receipts and provide documentation when requested.

---

This section outlines the standard practices for compensation and reimbursement at A&H Games. Employees are expected to follow these guidelines and communicate with management regarding any questions or concerns.
