# Code of Conduct

All employees of A&H Games are required to uphold the standards of conduct outlined in this section. These standards exist to ensure a safe, professional, and respectful environment for both employees and customers.

Failure to follow these standards may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

Lack of familiarity with these expectations does not exempt an employee from accountability.

---

## Expected Conduct

Employees are expected to conduct themselves in a manner that reflects the mission, values, and professionalism of A&H Games at all times while on shift or representing the company.

---

## Prohibited Conduct

The following actions are considered violations of the A&H Games Code of Conduct:

### Integrity & Financial Violations
- Theft or inappropriate removal or possession of company or customer property  
- Giving unauthorized or inappropriate discounts  
- Falsification of timekeeping or company records  
- Unauthorized use of company property  

---

### Substance Use
- Working under the influence of alcohol or illegal drugs  
- Possession, distribution, sale, or use of alcohol or illegal drugs in the workplace  

---

### Workplace Behavior
- Fighting or threatening violence  
- Harassment of any kind  
- Boisterous, disruptive, or inappropriate behavior  
- Insubordination or disrespectful conduct toward management, coworkers, or customers  

---

### Safety & Compliance
- Violation of safety or health policies  
- Smoking or vaping inside the store  
- Negligence or improper conduct resulting in property damage  

---

### Attendance & Responsibility
- Absence or tardiness without proper notice  
- Failure to perform assigned duties at an acceptable level  

---

### Confidentiality & Policy Violations
- Unauthorized disclosure of confidential or sensitive information  
- Violation of company policies or procedures  

---

## Enforcement

Management reserves the right to determine whether specific actions fall within the scope of prohibited conduct.

Depending on the severity of the violation, disciplinary action may include:
- Verbal warning  
- Written warning  
- Immediate termination  

---

Maintaining these standards is essential to protecting the integrity of A&H Games, supporting your coworkers, and providing a positive experience for our community.
