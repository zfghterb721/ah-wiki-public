# Code of Conduct

All employees of A&H Games are required to follow the standards of conduct outlined in this section.

These standards define unacceptable behavior. Violations may result in disciplinary action, up to and including immediate termination.

Lack of familiarity with these standards does not exempt an employee from accountability.

---

## Prohibited Conduct

The following actions are violations of the A&H Games Code of Conduct:

---

### Integrity & Financial Violations

- Theft or inappropriate removal or possession of company or customer property  
- Giving unauthorized or inappropriate discounts  
- Falsification of timekeeping or company records  
- Unauthorized use of company property  

Any violation in this section may result in immediate termination.

---

### Substance Use

- Working under the influence of alcohol or illegal drugs  
- Possession, distribution, sale, or use of alcohol or illegal drugs in the workplace  

---

### Workplace Behavior

- Fighting or threatening violence  
- Harassment of any kind  
- Boisterous, disruptive, or inappropriate behavior  
- Insubordination or disrespectful conduct toward management, coworkers, or customers  
- Use of profanity or inappropriate language in the store  
- Failure to enforce store policies or allowing policy violations to occur  

---

### Safety & Compliance

- Violation of safety or health policies  
- Smoking or vaping inside the store  
- Negligence or improper conduct resulting in property damage  

---

### Attendance & Responsibility

- Absence or tardiness without proper notice  
- Failure to perform assigned duties at an acceptable level  

---

### Appearance & Representation

Employees represent A&H Games while on shift and when identifiable as an employee.

- Clothing must not display profanity, sexual content, or offensive imagery  
- Personal hygiene must be maintained to a level appropriate for a customer-facing environment  
- Strong or unpleasant odors (including smoke, alcohol, or marijuana) are not permitted while on shift  

Employees must present themselves in a way that supports a clean, professional, and family-friendly environment.

#### External Representation

Employees may not display content that conflicts with the store’s family-friendly environment when it reflects on the business.

This includes, but is not limited to:

- Clothing worn to or from work  
- Visible items while on shift  
- Vehicle decals or displays visible while parked at or near the store  

Management will determine whether specific content is inappropriate.

Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action.

Repeated violations of appearance or representation standards may result in termination.

---

### Confidentiality & Policy Violations

- Unauthorized disclosure of confidential or sensitive information  
- Violation of company policies or procedures  

---

## Enforcement

Management determines whether conduct violates this policy.

Disciplinary action may include:

- Verbal warning  
- Written warning  
- Immediate termination  

Severity, frequency, and impact of the violation will determine the outcome.
