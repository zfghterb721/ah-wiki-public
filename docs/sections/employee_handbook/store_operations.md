# Store Operations

This section outlines the policies that govern how employees operate within the store during their shifts. These policies are designed to ensure strong customer service, proper inventory handling, and a secure, well-managed environment.

---

## On-the-Clock Expectations

Employees are expected to remain attentive, available, and engaged with the store at all times while clocked in.

- Employees must prioritize customers, store awareness, and assigned responsibilities  
- Employees should remain in positions where they can assist customers quickly  
- Employees are expected to maintain awareness of both the retail area and play space  

While on shift, your role is to support the store and its customers—not to participate as a customer.

---

## Gameplay While on the Clock

To maintain a high level of service and store awareness, employees may not participate in gameplay while clocked in.

### Not Permitted
- Playing in tournaments or events  
- Casual gameplay away from the counter  
- Leaving assigned responsibilities to participate in games  

### Permitted
- Brief demonstration games at the counter to assist customers  
- Teaching gameplay mechanics to customers  
- Running and facilitating events  

Employees are encouraged to help customers learn games, but must remain focused on their responsibilities.

### Scheduling Around Events
Employees may not skip or avoid scheduled shifts to participate in events.

If you wish to participate in an event:
- Coordinate with your manager in advance  
- Ensure your shift is properly covered  

Being present in the store without working is not a substitute for attending your scheduled shift.

---

## Store Area Access

To maintain security and organization, the store is divided into employee-only and customer-accessible areas.

### Employee-Only Areas
- Behind the counter  
- Storage rooms  
- Inventory and operational spaces  

Customers are not permitted in these areas under any circumstances unless explicitly authorized by management.

---

### Customer Areas
- Retail floor  
- Play space  
- Restrooms  

Employees should ensure these areas remain clean, organized, and welcoming.

---

## Inventory Handling

Maintaining accurate and secure inventory is critical to store operations.

### General Expectations
- All inventory must be handled carefully and accounted for  
- Items may not be removed from inventory without proper processing  

### Personal Use of Inventory
- Employees may not use store inventory for personal purposes  
- This includes borrowing items for gameplay or events  

### Promotional Use
Inventory may be used for:
- Prize support  
- Demonstrations  
- Customer goodwill (with approval)  

All promotional usage must:
- Be approved when required  
- Be processed through the POS system  
- Be recorded using the appropriate "Promotional" designation  

Inventory used for promotional purposes must not end up in the personal possession of employees.

---

## Customer Interaction Expectations

Employees are expected to provide a helpful, professional, and welcoming experience for all customers.

- Greet customers when they enter the store  
- Offer assistance when appropriate  
- Maintain a friendly and respectful tone  
- Avoid ignoring customers while using phones or engaging in side conversations  

Employees should be approachable and available at all times during their shift.

---

## Store Condition and Cleanliness

Employees are responsible for maintaining a clean and organized store environment.

- Keep the counter area clean and uncluttered  
- Maintain organization of shelves and displays  
- Ensure play areas are kept orderly  
- Address spills or messes promptly  

A clean store reflects professionalism and improves the customer experience.

---

## After-Hours Policy

Employees and customers are not permitted to remain in the store after closing unless approved by management.

- All closing procedures must be completed before leaving  
- Doors must remain locked after closing  
- No unauthorized access is permitted after hours  

---

These operational policies ensure that A&H Games maintains a professional, secure, and customer-focused environment. Employees are expected to follow these guidelines at all times while on shift.
