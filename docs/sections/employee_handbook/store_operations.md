# Store Operations

This section defines how employees are required to operate while on shift at A&H Games.

These policies ensure consistent customer service, proper inventory handling, and a secure, well-managed store environment.

Failure to follow these policies may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination.

---

## On-the-Clock Expectations

Employees must remain attentive, available, and engaged at all times while clocked in.

- Prioritize customers, store awareness, and assigned responsibilities  
- Remain in positions where customers can be assisted quickly  
- Maintain awareness of both the retail area and play space  

Employees are working while on shift. Personal activity must not interfere with responsibilities.

---

## Gameplay While on the Clock

Employees may not participate in gameplay while clocked in.

### Not Permitted

- Playing in tournaments or events  
- Casual gameplay away from the counter  
- Leaving responsibilities to participate in games  

### Permitted

- Brief demonstration games at the counter to assist customers  
- Teaching gameplay mechanics to customers  
- Running and facilitating events  

Employees must remain focused on their responsibilities at all times.

Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action.

---

## Scheduling Around Events

Employees may not skip or avoid scheduled shifts to participate in events.

If an employee wishes to participate in an event:

- Coordinate with a manager in advance  
- Ensure the shift is covered and approved  

Being present in the store as a customer does not replace a scheduled shift.

---

## Store Area Access

The store is divided into employee-only and customer-accessible areas.

### Employee-Only Areas

- Behind the counter  
- Storage rooms  
- Inventory and operational spaces  

Customers are not permitted in employee-only areas under any circumstances unless explicitly authorized by management.

Employees must enforce this policy.

---

### Customer Areas

- Retail floor  
- Play space  
- Restrooms  

Employees must maintain these areas in a clean, organized, and welcoming condition.

---

## Inventory Handling

Maintaining accurate and secure inventory is critical.

### General Requirements

- All inventory must be handled carefully and accounted for  
- Items may not be removed from inventory without proper processing  

### Personal Use of Inventory

- Employees may not use store inventory for personal purposes  
- This includes borrowing items for gameplay or events  

### Promotional Use

Inventory may be used for:

- Prize support  
- Demonstrations  
- Customer goodwill (with approval)  

All promotional usage must:

- Be approved when required  
- Be processed through the POS system  
- Be recorded using the appropriate designation  

Inventory used for promotional purposes must not enter personal possession.

Unaccounted inventory may be treated as theft.

---

## Customer Interaction

Employees must provide a helpful, professional, and responsive experience at all times.

- Greet customers when they enter the store  
- Offer assistance when appropriate  
- Remain available and approachable  
- Do not ignore customers while using phones or engaging in side conversations  
- Address inappropriate or disruptive behavior from customers when it occurs

Employees must actively monitor the store and respond to customer needs.

---

## Store Condition and Cleanliness

Employees are responsible for maintaining the store during their shift.

- Keep the counter area clean and uncluttered  
- Maintain organization of shelves and displays  
- Keep play areas orderly  
- Address spills or messes immediately  

Do not leave cleanup tasks for the next shift.

---

## After-Hours Policy

Employees and customers are not permitted in the store after closing without management approval.

- Complete all closing procedures before leaving  
- Keep doors locked after closing  
- Do not allow unauthorized access after hours  

Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action.

---

These policies define required behavior while operating the store. Employees are expected to follow them at all times while on shift.
