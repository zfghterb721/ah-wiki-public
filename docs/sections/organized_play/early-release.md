# Early Release Guidelines for Trading Card Games

## Magic: The Gathering

- **Early Release Date:** Can sell all products and singles starting on the prerelease date.

## Pokemon

- **Early Release Date:**
  - Can sell booster boxes, and booster packs the Monday before release and after you have run a prerelease event.[[1]](https://support.pokemon.com/hc/en-us/articles/20981683125524-Prerelease-Program-FAQ#:~:text=What%20Is%20the%20Early%20Release%20Program)
  - Specialty sets do not have prereleases so there are no early releases for these sets.
  - You are only allowed to sell leftover Prerelease products after the release date for the set. Failure to adhere to these rules may result in loss of Prerelease eligibility and/or disciplinary action against your League.[[2]](https://support.pokemon.com/hc/en-us/articles/20981683125524-Prerelease-Program-FAQ#:~:text=Sale%20of%20Prerelease%20Kits&text=You%20are%20only%20allowed%20to,disciplinary%20action%20against%20your%20League.)

## Yugioh

- **Early Release Date:** Three days prior to release.
- **Premier Events:** Can also sell packs a week prior during premier events.

## Bandai

- **Early Release Date:** Can sell booster boxes on the day of prerelease.
