This set of rules is for the Set Championship: Into the Inklands. Rules for subsequent Championships may be subject to change. It is **your** responsibility to check on these rules and make sure they are being followed. Updates for following Championships may be found [here.](https://www.disneylorcana.com/en-US/resources/)

# Prizes

In order for a Championship event to take place, including awarding prizes, at least eight players must be present, have a tournament-legal deck, and make a good-faith effort to play at least one full round. If you do not have eight players who meet these requirements, you must cancel your event and return this package and its contents to your local Ravensburger office.

Stores must award kit prizes as described below so that it is consistent with our messaging to players. Stores are strongly encouraged to provide additional prizes for their events, though it is not required. Generally, how additional prizes are to be awarded should be published prior to the event and announced at the beginning of the event. Cash prizes are not allowed.

- **Champion:** The winner receives:
  - 1 Store Champion Playmat
  - 1 Stitch – Rock Star promo card
- **Runner-Up:** The second-place finisher receives:
  - 1 Stitch — Rock Star Playmat (non-champion version)
  - 1 Stitch – Rock Star promo card
- **Third and Fourth Place** finishers each receive:
  - 1 Stitch – Rock Star promo card

# Tournament Details

- **Format:** Core (Constructed)
- **Rounds:** 50 minutes, best of three games (Top Cut matches are untimed)
- **Structure:** Swiss Rounds and possible Top Cut, depending on attendance

A judge is required for this event; that person cannot play in the tournament. Stores must provide a person experienced in judging trading card game events and playing the Disney Lorcana Trading Card Game. A certified judge is preferable although not required.

Players must register the deck that they are going to use in the Championship. The sheet they must fill out can be found [here.](https://cdn.ravensburger.com/lorcana/op-constructed-decklist-registration-sheet)

Stores are required to run their event using the Melee platform. If you have any questions, please contact us at lorcanaop@ravensburger.com.
