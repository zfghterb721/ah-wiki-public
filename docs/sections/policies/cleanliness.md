# Store Cleanliness Policy

A&H Games is committed to maintaining a clean, organized, and welcoming environment for all customers and employees.

Cleanliness is a shared responsibility between employees and customers. Employees are expected to actively maintain store conditions and address issues as they arise.

---

## General Expectations

The store should remain clean and orderly at all times.

This includes:
- Clean retail and counter areas  
- Organized shelves and displays  
- Tidy and usable play space  
- Clear walkways and seating areas  

Employees should address cleanliness issues as soon as they are noticed.

---

## Customer Responsibilities

Customers are expected to:

- Dispose of trash in designated bins  
- Keep play areas reasonably clean  
- Avoid leaving personal items unattended  
- Clean up after themselves when possible  

Food, drinks, and gameplay materials should not create excessive mess or disruption.

---

## Employee Responsibilities

Employees are responsible for maintaining overall store cleanliness.

This includes:
- Keeping the counter area clean and uncluttered  
- Regularly checking and clearing trash  
- Straightening product displays and shelves  
- Monitoring play areas for messes or spills  

Employees should take initiative rather than waiting for issues to escalate.

---

## Food and Drink

Food and drink are permitted in the store, but must be handled responsibly.

- Customers should avoid spilling food or drinks  
- Messes should be cleaned promptly  
- Employees may ask customers to clean up after themselves if necessary  

If a spill or hazard occurs, employees should address it immediately.

---

## Restrooms

Restrooms should be maintained in a clean and usable condition.

Employees should:
- Check restrooms periodically  
- Report issues that require attention  
- Address minor cleanliness issues when possible  

---

## Addressing Cleanliness Issues

Employees may address cleanliness concerns directly with customers.

- Approach situations politely and professionally  
- Clearly communicate expectations  
- Request that customers clean up their area when appropriate  

**Suggested response:**  
"Hey, could you help us out by cleaning that up? We try to keep the space clean for everyone."

---

## Escalation

If a customer refuses to follow reasonable cleanliness expectations:

- Reinforce the request clearly  
- Involve a manager if necessary  
- Continued refusal may be treated as a customer conduct issue  

---

Maintaining a clean store is essential to creating a positive experience for our community. Employees are expected to take ownership of the store environment and address cleanliness issues proactively.
